Scheduling

More than 24 hours notice
Sessions may be cancelled or rescheduled at no charge. Prepayment is carried forward or refunded in full.
Less than 24 hours notice

No-show
If you're unable to attend, please let me know in advance — sessions missed without notice cannot be rescheduled.

Scope of work

A note on what this is — and isn't

Career advisory and coaching is a forward-looking, collaborative process focused on professional goals, decisions, and transitions. It is not a substitute for psychological counselling, legal advice, or financial planning.

If at any point it becomes clear that a different kind of support would serve you better, I will say so directly and, where possible, suggest appropriate resources.
